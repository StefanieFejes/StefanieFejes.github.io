Beach Volleyball

Beach volleyball has been a cornerstone of my life, shaping my values of discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Since the age of 15, I have proudly represented Australia at both junior and senior levels, competing around the world, achieving remarkable results, and creating meaningful connections along the way.

Goals & Aspirations

High performance sport is what I live for, I thrive off pressure, challenges and learning. I want to become the best beach volleyball player in the world.
Some of my major goals include:

  1. Qualify & achieve top 5 at 2025 World Championships in Adelaide
  2. Become a world top 10 ranked team
  3. Consistently medal at the highest world tour level (Challenge & Elite 16 events)
  4. Medal 2027 World Championships
  5. Qualify & achieve top 5 at LA 2028 Olympic Games
  6. Gold Medal 2032 Brisbane Olympic Games

Both on and off the court, I aim to inspire athletes and sports enthusiasts of all ages to dream big, build confidence, and advocate for important causes, including athlete well-being and community development. Connecting and giving back to the sports community has always been a passion and a priority in my journey.
